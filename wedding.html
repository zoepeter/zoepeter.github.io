The Wedding

Dress Code

The ceremony and reception will be held outdoors on grass. We encourage you to bring something to keep you warm and recommend against wearing stilettos.

Facilities

Please note that there are no toilets at Burbrook Forest. Toilets are available at Kuitpo Hall, which you can use before or after the ceremony.

No smoking in the forest please.

Unplugged Ceremony

We would love you to thoroughly enjoy your time at Burbrook Forest, so we respectfully ask that you turn off your phones and do not take photographs during the ceremony. We have professional photographers who will be there to capture every moment. Please feel free to use your phones and cameras at the reception to capture the fun of the evening.
