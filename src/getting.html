Directions

Please arrive at Burbrook Forest, 855 Brookman Rd, Kuitpo, by 3:15pm for a 3:30 start.



Shuttle Bus Information

We will organise a bus to ferry guests from the city to the venue and back.

  • The bus will depart from the Adelaide Convention Centre at 2pm.
  • After the ceremony, the bus can shuttle guests to Kuitpo Hall (approx 1 min drive).
  • The bus will depart from Kuitpo Hall around 11pm to return to the Adelaide Convention Centre.

If you are interested in taking the bus please let us know via the RSVP form. If you register your interest, we will contact you to with further information.

Parking

There is parking at both the ceremony and reception venues. Please see The Wedding for a map outlining parking locations.

Cars can be left overnight at the parking lot opposite Kuitpo Hall.

Taxis

Taxis and Ubers do not regularly service Kuitpo. If you would like a taxi it will need to be pre-booked.
